About me

Since I was very young, I knew I was meant for the educational field. It is one of the biggest ways that I have found which helps me achieve my calling of helping people in all capacities of life. After a lot of hard work and many walked roads, I can proudly say that I remain a driven educator after 5 years of experience teaching a variety of students in academic and cultural education. As a leader who manages and guides learning experiences for students and peers both on and offline, I know the importance of excellent interpersonal and collaborative skills in a successful learning and teaching environment. Over my short few years as an instructor, I have developed a strong understanding of in-person & online instruction, and advanced knowledge of adult learning theory. My personal take on learning is that it is crucial to never stop. I identify as a lifelong learner, and utilize that passion to always view the experiences I encounter in daily life as teachable moments. We learn best about the things we can personally connect with; the things we find fun and exciting. In my free time, you will likely find me exploring creative ventures in Web Design and Development, Instructional Design for E-learning, and learning about Self Development in leadership and management.

I specialize in: • Curriculum and Content Development • Educational Technology & Digital Literacy • Coaching/Training/Mentoring • Student Centered Learning • Project Management and Leadership • Academic Literacy Development
